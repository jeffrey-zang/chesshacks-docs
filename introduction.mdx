# Welcome to ChessHacks

ChessHacks is a competitive hackathon where you design, build, and deploy chess bots that battle against each other in automated matches.

## What is ChessHacks?

Create intelligent chess bots using any strategy or algorithm you choose, deploy them to our platform, and watch them compete on the leaderboard.
