title: Rules

Below is a general outline for building your project in a way that qualifies it to win prizes. For more information on what type of bots are allowed, see the [Project Overview](/competition/overview) page.

#### Your team

1. Teams must consist of 1-4 hackers who have been accepted to ChessHacks.
2. All team members must have checked-in at the venue during the designated check-in period. 

#### Your project

1. The entirety of the project must be made during the hacking period, which starts at 10:00 PM EST, November 14, 2025 and ends at 10:00 AM EST, November 16, 2025.

2. The project must not be submitted to any other hackathons. 

3. You may not submit multiple projects. You can submit to multiple challenges for the same project. 

4. You project code must be in a public GitHub repository.
