## Queen's Crown

This prize is awarded to the best overall model (highest ELO) at the end of the hackathon.

Prize: **$1000**

## Bishop's Gambit

We've selected one of Waterloo's best (human) chess players for this challenge. This prize is awarded to the bot that is the first to defeat them in a match. 

Prize: **$1000**

## Rook's Rampage

King of the hill other bots for the highest time spent at the top! This prize is awarded to the bot that spends the most time at the highest ELO rating throughout the hackathon.

Prize: **$1000**

## Knight's Edge

This prize rewards keeping your chess model small, under 10MB. It will be awarded to the bot that has the highest ELO while being under 10 megabytes in size.

Prize: **$1000**

## Pawn's Rebellion

This prize is awarded to the team that is the first to beat our baseline chess model.

Prize: **$1000**
